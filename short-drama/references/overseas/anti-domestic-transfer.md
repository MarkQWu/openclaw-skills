# Anti-Domestic Transfer

Purpose: block Chinese domestic short-drama mechanisms, male-frequency genre logic, and domestic skill habits from leaking into overseas vertical drama.

## 5 C-Drama Red Flags

These are plot mechanisms that Chinese screenwriters default to but English mafia romance readers will reject.

### Red Flag 1: Lab-Report Misunderstanding Engine

**Pattern**: Fatal toxin + wrong blood report -> drives 10 episodes of misunderstanding.

**Why it fails**: English audiences consider lab-report reveals "Grey's Anatomy cheap" since ~2010.

**Replacement**: Identity misrecognition beats: she thinks he is the informant because they share a tattoo; he thinks she is undercover because she accessed a specific file.

### Red Flag 2: Aphrodisiac / Cold Medicine Mix-Up

**Pattern**: Chemical substance accidentally causes intimacy.

**Why it fails**: English dark romance intimacy is gated by "choice under pressure." Chemical accident creates a review-risk pattern: coercive intimacy without authored choice or payoff.

**Replacement**: Delete entirely. Use forced proximity: safe house with one bed, hiding from pursuit, or power outage in a storm shelter.

### Red Flag 3: 追妻火葬场 Without English Grovel Grammar

**Pattern**: Male public humiliation -> female cold indifference -> male grovels with flowers/gifts.

**Why it fails**: English readers have specific grovel expectations.

**Required grammar**:

1. He does what she always asked but he refused.
2. He gives up what he swore never to give up.
3. He chooses her over family at literal gunpoint.

**Treatment**: Keep the arc, rewrite every beat to English grovel grammar.

### Red Flag 4: 下克上 Without English Power Mapping

**Pattern**: Young subordinate overthrows patriarch.

**Status**: Clean transfer if mapped to the Tommy Shelby (Peaky Blinders S1-2) "young capo rising" model.

**Danger zone**: If the power dynamics rely on audience understanding Chinese hierarchy (入赘/血脉), it fails.

### Red Flag 5: Good Girl -> Mafia Queen Without Moral Turning Point

**Pattern**: Female lead "becomes powerful" but never makes a morally irreversible choice.

**Why it fails**: Without a moral turning point episode she is just "a sad girl in a nice dress" -- the classic 霸总 failure mode.

**Useful direction**: Give her one morally irreversible choice that changes the plot and reveals what darkness she is willing to own. Violent examples must stay inside `hard-rules.md` violence calibration and `compliance-risk.md` target-market boundaries; the requirement is irreversible agency, not a required hit/torture beat.

## Cross-Case Failure Pattern: Male-Frequency Content Has High Transfer Risk

The 2023-2024 Chinese source cluster reported repeated overseas failures for 赘婿 / 战神 / 都市玄幻 style projects across DramaBox / ShortMax / FlexTV / GoodShort. Treat this as strong transfer-risk evidence, not as a platform-official statistic.

Working diagnosis: not just execution quality, but **genre mismatch**. Chinese male-frequency satisfaction arcs (humiliation -> power reversal) have weak target-market equivalence when exported literally. Female-frequency genres (霸总/狼人/复仇) have shown better overseas fit in the same source cluster.

**Implication**: Mafia romance is female-frequency content: female POV watching a dangerous male lead. It is a tailwind genre, not a headwind. This genre direction is fundamentally correct for English-language vertical drama.

## Domestic Skill Carryover Failures

The following elements from the domestic short-drama skill are anti-patterns for overseas use. They must not leak into this skill:

1. **红果 160+ self-check rubric**: built for Chinese domestic platform, actively harmful for English audiences.
2. **爽点 density scoring**: replaced by romance beat emotional threshold.
3. **打脸/装X/反转 as KPIs**: English audiences read these as petty or juvenile.
4. **Domestic 4-stage wave pattern**: replace with target-market relationship pressure, reveal/reversal function, and paywall pressure. Gwen Hayes may assist privately but is not the governing platform structure.
5. **"Physical conflict can only appear once" rule**: reversed for mafia romance. Male lead violence toward threats to the female lead is a recurring, expected beat.
6. **Single female POV assumption**: overseas dark romance defaults to dual POV.

## AI-Generated Drama Quality Collapse

127,800 AI short dramas -> 0.117% broke 100M views, versus roughly 2-3% for human-produced work in the same source cluster. The pattern is traffic arbitrage, not storytelling.

**Implication**: The market top (DramaBox/ReelShort) explicitly positions "human-written" as a selling point. AI-written scripts must be indistinguishable from human-written scripts, which means passing craft rubrics for beat density, emotional curve, dialogue rhythm, motivation coherence, and scene causality.

**Compliance boundary**: unauthorized likeness, source resemblance, and synthetic-person exposure belong in `compliance-risk.md`, not here.
